Bio

Hi — I'm a student of markets.

I'm an undergraduate studying Accounting & Finance, with a research habit that started long before my first class. What began as curiosity about how businesses make money has turned into a daily practice — reading 10-Ks, modeling companies in Excel, and writing notes to think out loud.

My goal is to become a thoughtful capital allocator. The path I'm choosing for now: build deep fundamentals in accounting, sharpen judgment through writing, and stay technically literate as software eats every industry I want to invest in.

Outside of school I read across finance, psychology, and tech, keep a personal knowledge garden, and run experiments — small portfolios, models, dashboards — to learn by doing.
